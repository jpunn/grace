s it possible to experience abundance of peace in a Christian life?

I would say YES if we abide in Christ. Although conflicts or confusion arises in our life journey, as Christians, we definitely find peace in our Savior.We have strong shoulders to lean on, which is of our Heavenly Father who reigns on the throne, who has all the supremacy. He wants us to have the same peace He has . It is not impossible for a child of God to reflect His image. We never see Son of Man in stress or in nervousness in any scenes. Jesus was peacefully sleeping in the boat when the disciples were terrified by the storm. He did not lose His control even when He was tempted by satan, or even in Gethsemane.

 You might say he is God and we are humans. However, Jesus could experience all the feelings while He lived on this earth as Son of Man. He truly experienced the thirst, hunger, pain, etc. But He had uninterrupted communication with His Father in Heaven at all times. He separated himself at multiple occasions to spend time with His Father. The prayer at Gethsemane undoubtedly helped him to take the Cross and fulfill His mission of the redemption of man kind. Our redeemer is willing to equip us to overcome the challenges we face in our day to day life. Trust Him. He will make it. 

Philippians 1:6

God promised His people abundance of peace, the peace that surpasses all understanding. In Isaiah 66 , God compares peace to river. Sitting at river banks or beaches and watching the flowing water give peace of mind to many people. How much more peace would one experience in the presence of  our God who promised us to give the "living water".

Let's find true peace, His peace, the Peace in abundance.
